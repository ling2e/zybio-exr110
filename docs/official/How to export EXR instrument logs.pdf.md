# How to export EXR instrument logs.pdf

#### How to export instrument logs

1.Prepare a FAT32 formatted USB disk and insert it into the instrument USB port.

1. Log in to the engineer account. Account name: service，Password: mar@zybek.
3.Enter “Manage” interface. Select the “Service” tab. Click “One-click export”.

4.Select the required date and content, click "OK" to export to the USB drive.
